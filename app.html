Map Data & Accuracy
The information displayed on Cut2Fill is provided for general planning purposes only and should not be relied upon as a substitute for professional advice. Facility locations, boundaries, and zone data are approximate and may not reflect current conditions. Always verify critical information directly with the relevant facility operator, local council, or regulatory authority before making decisions.

Delivery Estimator
Fuel consumption, truck hours, and emissions estimates are indicative only, based on generalised vehicle profiles and straight-line distance calculations with a road correction factor. Actual values will vary based on route, terrain, traffic, vehicle condition, load configuration, and driving behaviour. Estimates use National Greenhouse Account (NGA) Factors 2024 for CO₂-e calculations.

Biosecurity Zones
Fire ant biosecurity zone boundaries shown are simplified representations of Queensland Government data. For official zone checks and Biosecurity Instrument Permit (BIP) requirements, always consult the Queensland Government Fire Ant Zone Checker.
