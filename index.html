Circular Economy

Every cubic metre reused is one
that doesn't go to landfill

Material Sitting Idle

Millions of cubic metres of usable fill are stockpiled, dumped, or buried every year across Queensland — simply because there's no easy way to connect it with projects that need it.

Untapped Value

Your excess cut isn't waste — it's a product. With the right geotechnical classification, material that costs you to dispose of becomes something you can sell. Testing pays for itself.

"General Fill" Means Nothing

Without proper classification, buyers don't know what they're getting and sellers can't prove what they have. Cut2Fill standardises material data so both sides can trade with confidence.

Who It's For

One platform,
four ways to use it

Finding Fill

Browse verified fill sources on the map — filter by material type, distance, and availability. See what's open now and what's coming online. Know what you're getting before you send a truck.

Selling Fill

Turn your excess material into a commodity. Get it tested, get it classified, list it on the platform. Cut2Fill connects you with geotech providers and gives your material a verified profile that buyers trust.

Managing Your Own Sites

Trucking companies and large operators — run Cut2Fill as your own internal platform. List private sites, manage your fleet's fill sources, and track material movement without going public.

Connecting Haulage

Some sites have fill but no trucks. Some projects need fill but no fleet. Cut2Fill helps trucking companies find work by connecting them with sites that need material moved.

Platform Features

The intelligence layer
that construction is missing

Material Classification

Standardised geotechnical classifications — not "general fill". Buyers know exactly what they're getting, sellers can prove exactly what they have. Material becomes a commodity, not a gamble.

Geotech Testing Framework

We connect you with testing providers and standardise what tests are needed. Spend the money on classification and it pays for itself — verified material sells, unverified material gets dumped.

Fire Ant Zone Mapping

Biosecurity zones overlaid on every site. Know your compliance requirements before you move a single cubic metre.

Live Site Status

Sites update in real time — closed after rain, capacity changes, access restrictions. No more wasted trips to sites that aren't accessible.

Haul Distance Calculator

Instantly see cartage distances and estimated costs from any fill source to your project site.

Vetted Listings

Every submitted site goes through our verification process before it goes live. Quality you can trust.
