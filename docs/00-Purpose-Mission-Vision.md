# Cut2Fill — Purpose, Mission & Vision

**Version:** 1.0 (DRAFT)
**Date:** 7 April 2026

---

## Purpose — Why We Exist

Queensland's construction industry moves millions of tonnes of earth every year with almost no visibility into where material is, what's available, or whether it meets compliance requirements.

The result: reusable material goes to landfill at $125/tonne. Projects overpay for virgin quarry material. Regulators can't track material flows. And 571,000 tonnes of reusable fill gets buried every year because nobody knew it was there.

**Cut2Fill exists to make construction earthworks visible.**

---

## Mission — What We Do

We map, classify, and connect Queensland's construction material supply chain — giving every site PM, estimator, contractor, and quarry operator instant visibility into what's available, where it is, and whether it's compliant.

We do this by:
- **Mapping** every quarry, landfill, transfer station, and treatment facility in SEQ
- **Classifying** materials against QLD regulatory standards so compliance is built in
- **Connecting** people who have material with people who need it — in 30 seconds, not 30 phone calls

The platform is free. The intelligence is the product.

---

## Vision — Where We're Going

A Queensland construction industry where no reusable material goes to landfill because nobody knew it existed.

Where every material movement is verified, every compliance requirement is met automatically, and the data generated helps government plan better infrastructure.

Cut2Fill becomes the intelligence layer that construction has never had — the first place anyone looks when they need to move earth.

## Values

### Build first, talk later
We ship product, not pitch decks. The platform speaks for itself.

### Free means free
Browsing, posting, and finding material is free. Permanently. The construction industry doesn't need another paywall — it needs visibility.

### Compliance is invisible
Users don't think "this platform checks my fire ant zones." They think "that was easy." The intelligence is built in, not bolted on.

### Every excavator, every quarry
A PM with one machine feels just as welcome as BMD Group. The platform serves the industry, not a segment of it.

### The data is the moat
604 verified facilities. Fire ant zone integration. Material classification. This can't be scraped or replicated overnight.
