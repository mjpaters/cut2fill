## 18:00 — Strategic analysis: payment facilitation + revenue model + competitors

### Competitor research
- **Rubbl (rubbl.com)** is NOT a materials marketplace — it's equipment rental (Airbnb for excavators). Not a competitor. The Australian RUBBL Technologies (rubbl.app) does landscape materials but is a different entity.
- **Soil Connect** deep dive in progress — key US competitor, $11.9M funded, 12,000+ users, 40M yards matched.

### The payment problem (critical strategic insight)
- Contractors can't easily pay non-registered sources for material
- Haulage companies solve this by bundling material cost into per-m3 haulage rate with standard payment terms — they're providing a financing service disguised as a service contract
- Cut2Fill opportunity: become the invoicing/payment intermediary
- Three levels identified: (1) visibility only, (2) invoice facilitation with transaction fee, (3) full escrow/payment platform
- Level 2 is the sweet spot for organic growth — Cut2Fill invoices the contractor (registered business, ABN, standard terms), pays the source minus fee

### The "free" problem
- Business plan says Year 1 is free — this is not practically sustainable
- Reframing: free to explore and browse, revenue from suppliers and transaction facilitation
- "Free" means the user doesn't pay to find fill — but the platform has revenue paths from day 1

## 16:30 — Logo concept generation via Recraft (Round 1)

AI logo exploration using Recraft MCP tool — 30+ concepts generated across 4 rounds.

### Concept directions explored
1. **Terrain Exchange** (Concept A from brief) — interlocking terrain profiles, cut slope meeting fill slope
2. **The Flow** (Concept B) — abstract ascending/descending forms with exchange gap
3. **C2F Monogram** (Concept C) — lettermark combining C, 2, F

### What worked
- **recraftv2 icon style (pictogram, colored_shapes, outline)** produced the cleanest, most logo-appropriate results
- The **two-triangle hourglass** concept emerged as the strongest mark — inverted triangle (cut) over upright triangle (fill) with gap (platform) between
- The **CUT2FILL emblem stamps** (emblem_vintage, emblem_stamp substyles) validated the sage green + amber color palette

### What didn't work
- **vector_illustration** styles produced complex illustrations, not logo marks — wrong tool for logos
- Recraft ignores color specifications most of the time — generates in whatever palette it wants
- **logo_raster without substyle** kept hitting API errors on text-heavy prompts
- First two rounds were too literal/complex — needed much simpler, more abstract prompts

### Strongest outputs
- `5f85a230.svg` — Clean two-triangle hourglass with bar (best overall)
- `095e1e22.svg` — Two directional arrows exchange mark
- `db9383e3.svg` — Bold C2 monogram
- `879d435e.svg` — Terrain outline shapes
- `17209738.webp` — CUT2FILL diamond stamp (right palette)

### Key learnings
- Recraft works best for logos using recraftv2 icon style (pictogram/colored_shapes)
- logo_raster emblem substyles good for branded emblems, bad for clean wordmarks
- Need to describe shapes geometrically, not conceptually, for best results
