# Queensland Local Government Area Council Contacts

**Purpose:** Contact list for reaching council works/infrastructure departments about local material sources (borrow pits, gravel pits, quarries) not captured in state databases.

**Compiled:** March 2026
**Source:** Publicly available information from council websites, QLD Local Government Directory, and LGAQ.
**Note:** Works/infrastructure department direct lines are rarely published separately. For most councils, call the main number and ask for the works/infrastructure or engineering services department. Email addresses follow the pattern shown where known.

**Total:** 77 Local Government Areas

## Notes

1. **Ask for:** Works Manager, Director of Infrastructure, or Engineering Services when calling main numbers.
2. **Standard QLD council website pattern:** Most councils use `[name].qld.gov.au` format.
3. **Best time to call remote councils:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-3pm. Many remote councils have limited office hours, especially Friday afternoons.
4. **Some email addresses above are best-guess patterns** based on common council email conventions. Verify via the council website contact page if a specific address bounces.
5. **QLD Local Government Directory** (official source for verification): https://www.localgovernment.qld.gov.au/for-the-community/local-government-directory
6. **LGAM Knowledge Base** (email address reference): http://lgam.wikidot.com/queensland-council-e-mail-addresses
