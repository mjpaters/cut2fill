## Audit Notes

- TMR data is current as at **25 February 2026** (per spreadsheet header)
- TMR registration confirms products are approved for government road/infrastructure projects
- Not all quarries need TMR registration — many supply only private sector
- TMR registration is the strongest signal of a "real" commercial quarry operation
- Cross-reference with GeoResGlobe Mining Leases provides additional verification where state tenures exist
